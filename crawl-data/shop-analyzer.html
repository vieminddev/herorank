Overview

A full Etsy shop audit in seconds.

What it does

Shop Analyzer pulls a complete performance breakdown for any Etsy shop — sales estimates, listings, reviews, tags, categories, policies — so you can see exactly what's working and where the gaps are.

Why it matters

Audit your own shop to spot quick wins. Audit competitors to learn what's driving their sales. Either way, you stop guessing and start making decisions based on the same data top sellers already have.

Features

Every signal a shop sends.

Sales & revenue estimates

Monthly sales, monthly revenue, total sales, average price — and a percentile ranking against the rest of Etsy.

Tag & category insights

The exact tags and categories the shop uses most, ranked by frequency — see the patterns behind their listings.

Listings breakdown

Every active listing, sorted by score, sales, revenue, views, faves, or date — with thumbnails, pricing, and quick links to the analyzer.

Review distribution

Visual 1–5 star breakdown plus a feed of recent reviews, filterable by sentiment so you can find positive/neutral/negative quickly.

Review summary

A condensed summary of recent reviews so you understand the overall buyer experience without reading hundreds of comments.

Shop policies & messaging

Announcement, vacation message, sale message, payment / shipping / refund policies — all the customer-facing copy in one view.

Shop performance metrics

Active listings, sales per listing, faves, total reviews, review rate — all the operational stats to gauge a shop's health.

Share results

One click to copy a shareable link to the analysis — perfect for handing off to a co-founder, VA, or client.

Free, no signup needed

Run audits without an account. Upgrade for higher daily limits and unrestricted use.

Getting started

How to use it.

  1. 01

    Enter a shop name

    Paste any Etsy shop name or URL — your own shop or a competitor's.

  2. 02

    Read the overview

    Start with the headline stats — monthly sales, revenue, percentile rank — then look at the most-used tags and categories to see how they position themselves.

  3. 03

    Inspect listings & reviews

    Switch to the Reviews tab for the rating distribution and recent feedback. Click any listing to drill into the Listing Analyzer.

  4. 04

    Apply what you learn

    For your own shop: address the gaps. For competitors: borrow the strategies that are working — pricing, tags, photo style, listing structure. Don't copy; adapt.

Overview

Spot problem buyers before you ship.

What it does

Buyer Check shows how a buyer has interacted with other shops — anonymous seller reviews from the RankHero community plus the buyer's own Etsy review history — so you can make an informed decision before fulfilling an order.

Why it matters

A handful of repeat problem buyers can drain your time and trigger negative reviews on listings that ship perfectly. Buyer Check helps you spot the patterns early, so you avoid wasted effort and unnecessary headaches.

Features

Everything you need to vet a buyer.

Protect your shop

Screen customers before shipping. A reliable Karen Check alternative built for Etsy sellers — see who you're actually selling to.

Seller reviews

Anonymous reviews from other sellers about their direct experience with this buyer — the seller-side perspective Etsy itself doesn't surface.

Buyer review history

Browse the buyer's past Etsy feedback — average rating, distribution, and recent reviews — to spot patterns in their behavior.

Sentiment filter

Filter reviews by All / Positive / Neutral / Negative to read the most relevant feedback first — no scrolling through hundreds of reviews.

Instant results

Get the full picture in seconds — paste a username and we pull everything available, ready when you are.

Private & anonymous

All searches and reviews are anonymous. Buyers never know if you viewed their profile or rated them.

Community powered

Help other sellers by leaving your own reviews. The more sellers contribute, the more accurate the buyer database becomes.

Two perspectives

See how the buyer rates sellers (Etsy reviews, in orange) and how sellers rate the buyer (RankHero reviews, in teal) — two sides, one full picture.

Free, no signup needed

Run buyer checks without an account. Upgrade for higher daily limits and unrestricted use.

Getting started

How to use it.

  1. 01

    Find the buyer's username

    Open your Etsy order and click the buyer's name. Copy their Etsy profile URL — or just the username.

  2. 02

    Search for the buyer

    Paste the username or profile URL into the search bar and hit Search. Results appear in a few seconds.

  3. 03

    Review the results

    Look at the rating distribution, read seller reviews, and scan the buyer's Etsy review history for patterns.

  4. 04

    Make your decision

    Use the data to decide how to proceed. For concerning histories, consider tracking, insurance, or extra documentation. Leave your own anonymous review afterward to help other sellers.

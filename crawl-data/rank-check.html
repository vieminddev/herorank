Overview

Find out where your listings actually rank.

What it does

Rank Check shows the exact position your listings hold in Etsy search results for any keyword — a clear, unbiased view that doesn't get personalized by your own browsing history.

Why it matters

It's hard to tell how well a listing is performing just by looking at it. Rank Check tells you exactly where you appear in search, so you can stop guessing and focus your SEO work on changes that actually move the needle.

Features

Real ranking data, on demand.

Search any keyword

Check your ranking for any search term buyers might use to find products like yours — no limits on which keywords you can test.

Location-based results

Switch between US, UK, EU, and other markets to see how your listings rank for buyers in different countries.

Competitor analysis

View the top-ranking listings for any keyword — see who you're competing with and what's working in your niche.

Side-by-side metrics

See price, views, faves, and listing age for every result — your listings and the competition in one row-aligned view.

Instant results

Real-time ranking data in seconds. No waiting on overnight reports — make SEO decisions while your edits are still fresh.

Validate SEO changes

Test if title and tag updates are actually moving your search position — or if it's time to try a different angle.

Shop listings highlighted

Drill into any listing

Getting started

How to use it.

  1. 01

    Enter a keyword

    Type a search term that buyers might use to find your products — like "handmade candle" or "vintage jewelry."

  2. 02

    Add your shop name

    Enter your Etsy shop name so we can pull your listings out and highlight where they rank in the results.

  3. 03

    Pick a location (optional)

    Choose a country to see how you rank for buyers in that region. Leave it on Global for an unbiased worldwide view.

  4. 04

    Review your rankings

    Compare your listings against the top results. Click any row to drill into the Listing Analyzer for specific optimization suggestions.

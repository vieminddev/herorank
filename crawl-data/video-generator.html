Overview

Listing videos shoppers actually watch.

What it does

Video Generator turns your product photos into a polished slideshow video — reorder slides, tune transitions and zoom, preview live, then export at the aspect ratio you need.

Why it matters

Etsy listings with video earn noticeably more attention from shoppers than photo-only listings. A short, professional clip lifts perceived quality and gives buyers more confidence at the moment they're deciding.

Features

Built to ship a listing video in minutes.

Live canvas preview

Every change is reflected instantly in the preview — no waiting on renders to see what your video looks like.

Drag-to-reorder slides

Drop in your product photos and drag them into the right order — the slide list updates the timeline as you move things around.

Style presets

Pick a starting style and you're 80% of the way there — every preset is tuned for Etsy listings, not generic stock-video looks.

Logo & positioning

Upload your shop logo, place it in any of nine positions, and tune opacity. Reinforce your brand on every frame without distracting from the product.

Multiple aspect ratios

Export square (1:1), landscape (16:9), or vertical (9:16) — match Etsy's listing video requirements or repurpose for Instagram and TikTok in one click.

Transitions & zoom

Subtle Ken Burns-style zoom and configurable transitions add motion without feeling gimmicky — Etsy buyers see polish, not a slideshow circa 2009.

Etsy-ready export

Output is encoded to match Etsy's listing video specs out of the box — duration, format, and resolution all hit the marks without manual tweaking.

Smart export checks

Before you download, we flag anything Etsy might reject — wrong aspect, video too long — and give you a one-click fix.

Free, no signup needed

Build and export videos within the daily free limit. Create an account or upgrade for higher daily limits.

Getting started

How to use it.

  1. 01

    Upload your photos

    Drop in product images — JPG, PNG, or WebP. Reorder them by dragging until the sequence tells the right story.

  2. 02

    Add a logo & pick a style

    Optionally upload your shop logo, position it, then choose a style preset and aspect ratio that fits where the video will run.

  3. 03

    Preview live

    Hit play to scrub through the video. Tweak transitions, zoom, and timing until everything feels right — every change updates instantly.

  4. 04

    Export & upload

    Click Download Video, wait a few seconds, and upload the file straight to your Etsy listing.

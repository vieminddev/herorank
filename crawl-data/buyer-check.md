Overview

## Spot problem buyers before you ship.

### What it does

Buyer Check shows how a buyer has interacted with other shops — anonymous seller reviews from the RankHero community plus the buyer's own Etsy review history — so you can make an informed decision before fulfilling an order.


### Why it matters

A handful of repeat problem buyers can drain your time and trigger negative reviews on listings that ship perfectly. Buyer Check helps you spot the patterns early, so you avoid wasted effort and unnecessary headaches.


Features

## Everything you need to vet a buyer.

### Protect your shop

Screen customers before shipping. A reliable Karen Check alternative built for Etsy sellers — see who you're actually selling to.

### Seller reviews

Anonymous reviews from other sellers about their direct experience with this buyer — the seller-side perspective Etsy itself doesn't surface.

### Buyer review history

Browse the buyer's past Etsy feedback — average rating, distribution, and recent reviews — to spot patterns in their behavior.

### Sentiment filter

Filter reviews by All / Positive / Neutral / Negative to read the most relevant feedback first — no scrolling through hundreds of reviews.

Get the full picture in seconds — paste a username and we pull everything available, ready when you are.

All searches and reviews are anonymous. Buyers never know if you viewed their profile or rated them.

### Two perspectives

See how the buyer rates sellers (Etsy reviews, in orange) _and_ how sellers rate the buyer (RankHero reviews, in teal) — two sides, one full picture.

Getting started

## How to use it.

1. 01


### Find the buyer's username



Open your Etsy order and click the buyer's name. Copy their Etsy profile URL — or just the username.

2. 02


### Search for the buyer



Paste the username or profile URL into the search bar and hit Search. Results appear in a few seconds.

3. 03


### Review the results



Look at the rating distribution, read seller reviews, and scan the buyer's Etsy review history for patterns.

4. 04


### Make your decision



Use the data to decide how to proceed. For concerning histories, consider tracking, insurance, or extra documentation. Leave your own anonymous review afterward to help other sellers.


FAQ

## Frequently asked questions.

Is this tool free to use without an account?

Yes — fully free, no account or credit card required. There's a daily limit on free use; create an account or upgrade for higher daily limits.

Will the buyer know I checked their profile?

No. Buyers are never notified when you use Buyer Check. Searches and reviews are completely confidential — there's no trace visible to the buyer.

What should I do if a buyer has negative reviews?

A few negatives don't automatically mean cancel — read them carefully. Often the right move is adding tracking, insurance, or photo documentation of your shipment. For very concerning histories, you can choose to cancel and refund.

Can I leave a review for a buyer?

Yes. After viewing a buyer's profile, click "Write a review" to share your experience anonymously. Your review helps other sellers and grows the community-powered buyer database.

What's the difference between RankHero reviews and Etsy reviews?

Etsy reviews (orange) = feedback the buyer left for sellers they purchased from. RankHero reviews (teal) = feedback sellers in the RankHero community left about the buyer. Together they give you both sides of the buyer's history.

Is Buyer Check a Karen Check alternative?

Yes. Buyer Check covers the same use case as Karen Check — screening buyers before fulfilling orders — and adds community-powered seller reviews, full buyer review histories, and a buyer rating system on top.

Can I cancel an order based on Buyer Check results?

You can — but use it as one signal among many. Etsy allows seller cancellations, but frequent cancellations can hurt your shop. Document your reasoning and follow Etsy's cancellation policies.

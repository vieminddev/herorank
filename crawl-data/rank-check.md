Overview

## Find out where your listings actually rank.

### What it does

Rank Check shows the exact position your listings hold in Etsy search results for any keyword — a clear, unbiased view that doesn't get personalized by your own browsing history.


### Why it matters

It's hard to tell how well a listing is performing just by looking at it. Rank Check tells you exactly where you appear in search, so you can stop guessing and focus your SEO work on changes that actually move the needle.

FAQ

## Frequently asked questions.

Is this tool free to use without an account?

Yes — fully free, no account or credit card required. There's a daily limit on free use; create an account or upgrade for higher daily limits.

What does "rank" mean?

Rank is the position your listing holds in Etsy search results for a given keyword — 1 means first, 10 means tenth, and so on. Lower numbers = more visible.

Why might rank differ from what I see on Etsy?

Etsy personalizes your own search results based on your history, location, and browsing behavior. Rank Check shows the unbiased typical-buyer view, which is more useful for benchmarking SEO.

Does this tool work internationally?

Yes. Switch the location dropdown to see how your listings rank for buyers in the US, UK, EU, Canada, Australia, and India — useful if you sell into different regional markets.

How can I use this data?

Three core uses:

- Track SEO performance over time
- Benchmark against competitors in your niche
- Test optimization changes and verify they're working

FAQ

## Frequently asked questions.

Is this tool free to use without an account?

Yes — fully free, no account or credit card required. There's a daily limit on free use; create an account or upgrade for higher daily limits.

What aspect ratio should I export for Etsy?

For Etsy listing videos, square (1:1) is the safest choice — it displays consistently across the gallery and on mobile. Use 9:16 if you also plan to repurpose the video for Reels or TikTok, and 16:9 for landscape banners.

How long should an Etsy listing video be?

Etsy allows listing videos between 5 and 15 seconds. Around 8 seconds is a sweet spot — long enough to show the product from a few angles, short enough to keep buyers engaged. Our default duration is set there.

How many photos should I include?

3 to 6 photos works best. Lead with your hero shot, follow with detail and scale shots, and end on something that conveys use or context. More photos than that and each slide gets too short to register.

Will Etsy accept the file format I export?

Yes. We export in MP4 (H.264) at 1080p — the format and resolution Etsy expects. Before download, the export checks flag anything Etsy might reject and offer a one-click fix.

Do my images leave my browser?

No. Video Generator runs entirely in your browser — your photos are processed locally and the video is rendered on your machine. Nothing is uploaded to our servers.

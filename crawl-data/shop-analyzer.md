Overview

## A full Etsy shop audit in seconds.

### What it does

Shop Analyzer pulls a complete performance breakdown for any Etsy shop — sales estimates, listings, reviews, tags, categories, policies — so you can see exactly what's working and where the gaps are.


### Why it matters

Audit your own shop to spot quick wins. Audit competitors to learn what's driving their sales. Either way, you stop guessing and start making decisions based on the same data top sellers already have.


Features

Monthly sales, monthly revenue, total sales, average price — and a percentile ranking against the rest of Etsy.

Active listings, sales per listing, faves, total reviews, review rate — all the operational stats to gauge a shop's health.

FAQ

## Frequently asked questions.

Are the sales numbers exact?

They're estimates derived from publicly available signals (reviews, favorites, Etsy's API). Treat them as directional — great for relative comparison and trend-spotting, not exact financials.

Can I analyze any shop, including competitors?

Yes. The data we use is all publicly visible on Etsy. Use it to audit your own shop, study competitors, validate niches, or just get inspired by what successful shops look like under the hood.

What does the percentile ranking mean?

"More sales than X%" tells you where the shop sits in Etsy's overall sales distribution. 90% means it sells more than 90% of shops on the platform — useful for benchmarking quickly.

Why are tags and categories useful?

They reveal how a shop positions itself in Etsy search. Patterns in the most-used tags tell you which keywords are driving discoverability — apply the same approach (with your own products) to compete in the same niches.

How do I read the review distribution?

Each row shows what % of reviews fell at that star level. A heavy concentration in 5-star with very few low-star reviews signals consistent quality. If you're seeing meaningful 1–3 star activity, scan those reviews to find the recurring pain points.

Can I use this for keyword research?

Indirectly — yes. The most-used tags surface the language a successful shop uses. For dedicated keyword research with search volume + competition data, jump over to the Tag Generator or Keyword Generator.

How fresh is the data?

Listings, reviews, and basic shop info come from live Etsy APIs. Sales estimates are recomputed regularly. Treat the snapshot as recent (within the last day) rather than literal real-time.

What if a shop is on vacation?

A vacation badge appears next to the shop name and the listings won't be available to buyers. Historical sales/review data is still shown, but recent activity will look paused.

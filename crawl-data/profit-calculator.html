Overview

Know your true earnings, after every fee.

What it does

Profit Calculator factors in every Etsy fee — listing, transaction, payment processing, regulatory, and Offsite Ads — so you see what you actually take home from each sale, not just the sticker price.

Why it matters

Etsy's fee structure adds up faster than most sellers realize. Get pricing wrong by even a few percent and your margin evaporates. Use this to price your products with confidence — and avoid underselling your work.

Features

Every Etsy fee, accounted for.

Location-specific fees

Pick your selling location — fees, currency, and payment-processing rates auto-adjust to the market you're actually selling in.

Full fee breakdown

See every Etsy fee individually — listing, transaction, payment processing, regulatory, Offsite Ads — so you understand exactly where the money goes.

Profit & margin output

Total profit, total fees, and profit margin percentage — all updated the moment you click Calculate.

Offsite Ads modeling

Toggle between 0%, 12%, and 15% Offsite Ads fees so you can model with-ads vs. without-ads scenarios for every listing.

Visual breakdown

A stacked bar shows what share of revenue goes to profit, costs, and fees at a glance — easy to spot when fees are eating margin.

Free, no signup needed

Run as many calculations as you need without an account or credit card. Built for everyday pricing decisions.

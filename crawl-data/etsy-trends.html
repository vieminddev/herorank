Overview

Spot Etsy demand before it peaks.

What it does

Etsy Trends surfaces rising search terms, breakthrough keywords, and upcoming holidays so your listings stay aligned with what shoppers are actively searching for — instead of where the market was last quarter.

Why it matters

Shopper interests shift fast. Reacting late to a trend means competing with everyone who got there first. Etsy Trends keeps you a step ahead — listings ready when buyers start searching, not weeks after.

Features

Every signal of where the market is moving.

Trending searches

The most popular Etsy search terms over the last 30 days, ranked by current volume.

Breakthrough keywords

Rapidly rising terms that weren't trending last month — early opportunities before the markets get crowded.

Fastest climbers & fallers

Two side-by-side leaderboards showing which terms gained or lost the most ranks since last month.

Upcoming holidays

A scrollable holiday ticker so you can plan seasonal products and campaigns weeks ahead of buyer demand.

Rank change indicators

Green and red arrows show whether each search term moved up or down in rank — instantly visible momentum.

Location targeting

Switch between US, UK, EU, and other markets to see what buyers in each region are actually searching for.

Real-time updates

Trend data refreshes regularly so the picture you see is recent, not last quarter's archive.

Paginated tables

Browse through hundreds of trending terms a page at a time without losing context.

Free, no signup needed

Browse the top trends without an account. Upgrade for the full database and unrestricted access to every page.

Getting started

How to use it.

  1. 01

    Pick a location

    Select a country to see trends for that market, or stay on Global for the worldwide picture.

  2. 02

    Scan the trending list

    Look at the top trending searches and watch the rank change arrows — momentum tells you which terms are heating up vs. cooling.

  3. 03

    Spot breakthrough opportunities

    Check breakthroughs and fastest climbers for fresh terms that haven't saturated yet — the best place to plant a flag early.

  4. 04

    Plan around holidays

    Use the upcoming holiday ticker to time seasonal products. Buyers shop weeks early — your listings should be ready before they start.

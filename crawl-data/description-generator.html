Story telling is emotional and narrative-driven, building a story around the product. Conversational is friendly and casual, like talking to a friend. Descriptive focuses on vivid sensory details and rich imagery. Pick what fits your brand.

We pick up colors, materials, patterns, and visual style cues from your photos so the generated copy matches what buyers actually see — usually a noticeable accuracy lift over text-only descriptions.

Short & sweet (3–5 sentences) suits simple products and quick-decision categories. Balanced (5–8 sentences) is the sweet spot for most listings. Keyword-stuffed (8–15 sentences) maximizes SEO coverage — best for complex or higher-priced items.

Add your most important keywords as focus tags (comma-separated) — typically 3 to 7. We weave them naturally through the description so SEO improves without sounding like a keyword dump.

A strong description covers what the product is, key features and benefits, materials and dimensions, use cases or occasions, care instructions where relevant, and your priority keywords woven naturally into the text.

It's better to generate a unique description per listing. Unique copy ranks better in search and feels more trustworthy to buyers. You can keep the structure consistent across listings, but vary the specific details for each product.
